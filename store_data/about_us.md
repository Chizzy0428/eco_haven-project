# About EcoHaven Mart

EcoHaven Mart is Nigeria’s #1 eco-friendly household store focused on promoting sustainable living.

**Location:** Lagos, Nigeria  
**Founded:** 2022  
**Mission:** Make sustainable living affordable and accessible.
